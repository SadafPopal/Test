Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1¾ cups flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat well.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into pans and bake for 30–35 mins.
  6. Cool and frost as desired.

Qabuli Palaw

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb or beef
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • Spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, salt)
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook meat with onions and spices until tender.
  2. Boil rice until half cooked, then drain.
  3. Layer meat and rice in a pot and steam.
  4. Fry carrots and raisins separately.
  5. Top the rice with carrots and raisins before serving.

Chicken Karahi

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp garlic-ginger paste
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and green chilies for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until thick.
  4. Simmer until chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and chilies.
